About

After working in the healthcare industry for a short time I saw it would not allow for movement in my career, and didn't fulfill my creative passion. I took up coding to make a simple passion project. I watched more and more tutorials, implemented new features, talked to people working in the tech industry and knew this would be a better fit for me.


Using AGILE methodology, I efficiently complete projects in a team. My goal is to keep both the client and future developers in mind. I create web apps that are responsive and accessible; while leaving organized code for myself and other devolopers to effortlessly adjust in future updates.
