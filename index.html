Me

I watched more and more tutorials, implemented new features, talked to people working in the tech industry and knew this would be a better fit for me.

My goal is to keep both the client and future developers in mind. I create web apps that are responsive and accessible; while leaving organized code for myself and other devolopers to effortlessly adjust in future updates.
